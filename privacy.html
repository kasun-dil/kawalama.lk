Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Welcome to Kawalama.lk. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web-based utility tools.

1. Client-Side Processing

Kawalama.lk is designed with privacy as a core principle. The majority of our tools (including Image to Text, PDF Watermark, QR Generator, and Exif Finder) operate entirely Client-Side. This means:

  • Your files (images, PDFs) are processed locally within your browser.
  • We do not upload your files to any external server.
  • We cannot see, access, or store the content of your documents.

2. Data Collection

Since we do not require user accounts, we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We prioritize anonymity.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's "Local Storage" to improve your user experience. This includes saving your preferences, such as:

  • Dark/Light Mode theme settings.
  • Recent history for the QR Generator (stored only on your device).

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache.

4. Third-Party Services

While we do not track you, we may use standard third-party services for website functionality, such as Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load fonts and icons faster.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
